1. COMMON GROUND: You MUST connect the Battery GND, Pi GND, Relay GND, and Servo GND together.

Servo Wire Color Function Connection

üíß B. Water Pump (via Relay)

The Relay acts as a switch. Logic Side connects to Pi, Load Side connects to Pump/Battery.

Logic Side (Low Voltage):
Relay VCC RPi 5V (Pin 2)
Relay GND RPi GND (Pin 9)
Relay IN RPi GPIO 23 (Pin 16)

Load Side (High Power):
Battery (+) To Pump (+) directly
Pump (-) To Relay COM pin
Battery (-) To Relay NO (Normally Open) pin

üöú C. Chassis Drive Motors (L298N Driver)

To move the robot, we use an L298N Motor Driver to control two DC motors (Left & Right).

L298N Module -> Power & Motors:
12V In Battery (+)
GND Common Ground (Battery - AND Pi GND)
5V Out Not Connected (If Pi has its own power)
OUT 1 & 2 Left Motor Wires
OUT 3 & 4 Right Motor Wires

L298N -> Raspberry Pi GPIO:
L298N Pin RPi GPIO Pin Function
IN1 GPIO 5 (Pin 29) Left Motor Fwd
IN2 GPIO 6 (Pin 31) Left Motor Rev
IN3 GPIO 13 (Pin 33) Right Motor Fwd
IN4 GPIO 19 (Pin 35) Right Motor Rev
ENA / ENB Keep Jumpers On Enable Motors (Max Speed)
